Note🏅 Awards

The Best Poster and Best Contributed Talk Awards recognise outstanding contributions to the workshop. We are particularly committed to highlighting the achievements of early-career researchers (within eight years of completion of PhD), including PhD students and postdoctoral fellows.

Award winners will be announced on the final day of the workshop. All submissions for contributed talks and posters by students and early-career researchers will automatically be considered for these awards.
